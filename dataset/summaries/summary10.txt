There are 3 rooms namely bedroom, washroom & drawing room. The drawing room contains one dining table, one 2 seater sofa, one square table & one rectangular sinc. The bedroom contains one bed and one square table. And the washroom contains one bathtub & one basin. There is main gate connected to the drawing room. And there are two more gates, one connects the drawing room and the bedroom and another one connects the drawing room and the bathroom. The drawing room is located in the middle of the bedroom and the washroom, the bedroom is located in south direction and the washroom is located in north direction.

There are 4 rooms namely bedroom, kitchen, washroom & drawing room. The drawing room contains one circular table and 3 single seater sofa. The bedroom contains 2 beds and one single seater sofa. The washroom contains one bathtub only. And the kitchen contains one dining table, one rectangular sinc and one basin. There is main gate connected to the drawing room. And there are 3 more gates, first one connects the drawing room and the kitchen, second one connects the drawing room and the bathroom and the last one connects the kitchen and the bedroom. The drawing room is located in the south-west direction, the bedroom is located in the north direction, the kitchen is located in the middle of the bedroom & the drawing room and the bathroom is located in the south-east direction.

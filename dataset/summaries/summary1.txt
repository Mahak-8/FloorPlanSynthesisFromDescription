There are two gates to enter into the house, both opening into the main hall and bedroom.

Left to the room is a door which opens up to a comparatively smaller room which has a dining table and sink in it. The main room has another door in upper front which opens up to an open area which has a door in its left connecting to a bathroom with a bathtub present in it

The bathroom is in the top-left side, dining hall in the bottom left side and the bedroom in the bottom right side.

It has a bathtub placed in one of its corner.

There are two single beds placed with a table between them and two sofas facing the bed. There are three rooms in a flat of two , one bathroom , a dining and a living room.

There are three door inside the house which joins drawing room and a sitting room, bed room and the sitting room and washroom and the sitting room.

The apartment has enterance connected to the hall. The left of the hall connects to bedroom  which has a bed and a chair. The right of the hall connects to the bathroom  which has a bathtub and a wash basin. 

The floor contains a living room, kitchen, bathroom and a bedroom. The Kitchen lies on the center right of the floor and contains a dining table and a sink. The living room is on the upper side and has a sofa and table on the left side as well as on the right side. The bedroom is on the lower left side and has a bed and a sofa. The bathroom is on the lower right side and  has a bathtub and a toilet seat.
